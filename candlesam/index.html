About PartyLite




CandleSam

I have been a PartyLite consultant for over 14 years and I love what I do. I started consulting in Anchorage, Alaska where I first found my passion for PartyLite and comfort candles. As time has gone on I have earned my business degree and IT skills to be more engaging in the digital world. I find that even in todays high tech world everyone can find a little comfort in the warmth that PartyLite candles and warmers bring to life. Living in Arizona now for over 7 years I have come to embrace Mesa as my new home and I have set off on my new mission to share my passion with the greater Phoenix community. I was shocked to find how much opportunity there is here from FlagStaff to Tucson! Everyone I meet has a story about how much they love the warmth of the candle glow and how important quality candles are to them. It is for this reason that I have made it my mission to share the news. I am here to help and PartyLite can bring those passions to reality. I want you to share your story with me and I will make your comfort candle dreams come true.
